Website Planning Document

Site Name

The site is going to be called "Always Ready."

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site "Always Ready" will be to keep the community and visitors informed in matters related to the local weather on the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Heaven located in Idaho State.

This will be accomplished by proviting up to date news about each town, weather tips, images, local activities to enjoy throughout the year, and the forecast for the local areas.

Domain Name

The site's domain name will be alwaysready.info, which can be purchased through domains.google.com for the price of $12/year.
