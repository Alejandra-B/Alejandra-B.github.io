Looking Forward!

We are proud to announce one of our most colorful and exciting events here in our beloved Preston City. On April 24 2021, from noon until 4 pm, we will celebrate our reach traditions with our yearly Hot Balloon Festival. Come ready to be amazed by the variety of colors and designs that will fill the skies. This activity will be a free event sponsored by our City Council members; come ready to enjoy a day at the park with friends and family and to make memories that will last for years to come.

Families, friends, and visitors from out of town gather year after year to spend an afternoon filled with entertainment and live music provided by some of our talented local musicians. We will also have vendors with delicious foods for purchase at low prices!

Don't miss this wonderful opportunity to strengthen bonds and friendship here in Preston City.The weather will be fantastic to spend it outdoors, start spreading the news and save the date!
