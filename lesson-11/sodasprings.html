Around the Corner!

For the past 10 years our beloved Soda Springs has been celebrating the coming of Spring with pot plants give away and a picnic at the main park followed by a parade. We've had a difficult last year with Covid-19 Pandemic, but we are ready to bring joy and color back to our community.

Families, friends, and visitors from out of town gather year after year to spend a day filled with joy and entertainment. Food will be provided by our local shop owners and the City council.

Don't miss this wonderful opportunity to strengthen bonds and friendship here in Soda Springs.

This activity will be held on Saturday April 3rd from 10:00am-4:00pm.The weather will be fantastic to spend it outdoors, start spreading the news and save the date!
