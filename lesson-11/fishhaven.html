Yeehaw Cowboy!

It's here once more! Our Rodeo weekend is back! We are proud to announce that Rodeo Weekend is coming up in 3 weeks. The weather will be espectacular in the coming weeks and we will spend them outdoors.

Families, friends, and visitors from out of town gather year after year to spend a day filled with the most entertaining activities! From BBQ to bareback riding, steer wrestling, tie-down roping, saddle bronc riding, team roping, barrel racing, bull riding, steer roping and the list goes on!! Food will be available to purchase, but feel free to bring your own grill, meat and pick up truck!

Don't miss this wonderful opportunity to strengthen bonds and friendship here in Fish Haven.

This activity will be held on Saturday April 10th and 11th from 8:00am-9:00pm.The weather will be fantastic to spend it outdoors, start spreading the news and save the date! Don't forget to wear masks and your cowboy boots!!
