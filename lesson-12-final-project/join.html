Why Join?

Joining Glendale Chamber of Commerce have many benefits:



  1. Make business contacts
  2. Receive chamber newsletters
  3. Acquire customer referrals
  4. Bring credibility to your business
  5. Increase your visibility in the community
  6. Networking opportunities
  7. Gain a voice in government

Membership Benefits by Level

$100 - Non-profit Level Ideal for new businesses, home-based businesses, or sole-proprietor without employees.

$200 - Bronze Level Ideal for businesses with 30 or fewer employees.

$400 - Silver Level Recommended for businesses with 200 or fewer employees.

$1,000 - Gold Level Recommended for businesses with more than 200 employees. Ideal for those planning to attend multiple Signature events and prefer to be billed for all events and benefits only once per year.
