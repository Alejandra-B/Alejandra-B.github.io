Our Purpose

Here in Glendale City Chamber of Commerce, we strive to support local businesses.

We find and provide tools, education and services to help our members achieved their potential and become a strong influence in our community.

We are a team of business owners and professionals working together to improve the quality of our local economy and public relations. Together we create a place of unity and networking.



GOALS

      Stand for the wellbeing of the local business environment by improving and promoting growth.
      Provide training and opportunities to interact with other business owners and gain knowledge and confidence.
      Foster activities that will help our members make network connections, contact with potential new clients, and help their business become a positive influence in our community.
      Provide resources that will help our local business environment grow.
      Facilitate our members with the tools to start making a strong impact and become a significant influence as a business owner and as a member of Glendale, Arizona!
